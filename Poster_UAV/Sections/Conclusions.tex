\section*{\titleA{CONCLUSIONS}}

\noindent
The results show an overall $R^2$ of 0.805, with an $RMSE$ of 0.208m. After evaluating the importance of each feature, the TPI with a radius of 250$px$ is by far the most valuable. While only the windward index for eastern winds appears important, all directional reliefs contribute to the final training outcome. The importance of features varies from chunk to chunk. So do the $R^2$ and $RMSE$ values.
