\section*{\titleA{SUMMARY}}

UAV surveys at the Zugspitze provided multi-temporal DSMs at 10\,cm resolution.
A snow depth model was derived by subtracting a snow-free summer DSM (Aug~2025) from a winter
DSM (Jan~2026). Several topographic indices were computed and used to train a Histogram Gradient
Boosting Regressor (HGBR), applied both globally and within spatial subsets (chunks).
